Cowboy Caviar

Cowboy Caviar is a mix of beans, corn, red onion, bell pepper, jalapeno, as well as various herbs and spices to be served with tortilla chips.

Ingredients

  • 1 can black eyed peas
  • 1 can black beans
  • 1 bag of frozen corns
  • 1/2 a red onion
  • 1 bell pepper
  • 2 jalapenos
  • 1/4 cup of olive oil
  • 1/4 cup of red win vinegar
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon of pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon of paprika
  • 1 teaspoon of chili powder
  • Lime juice to taste

Steps

  1. Thaw corn for about an hour prior to prep.
  2. Cut up bell pepper and jalapenos.
  3. Drain and rinse both cans of beans.
  4. Add all the veggies to a large bowl, mix thoroughly.
  5. In seperate bowl mix together all the wet ingredients to make a sauce.
  6. Add sauce to veggies and mix thoroughly.
  7. Serve with tortilla chips. Enjoy!
