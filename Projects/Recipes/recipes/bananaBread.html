Delicious Chocolate Chip Banana Bread.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup of butter
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 2 ripe bananas
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350℉
  2. Thoroughly coat the inside bottom and sides of a baking pan.
  3. Add butter and sugar to a large mixing bowl. Mix together thoroughly.
  4. Add bananas, break the bananas down to an apple sauce like consistency.
  5. Add eggs, thoroughly mix everything thats been added so far.
  6. Add flour, baking soda, and salt. Mix in slowly to avoid mess until a dough is formed.
  7. Add vanilla, mix well.
  8. Add chips, mix thoroughly into the dough.
  9. Bake for 55mins.
  10. Let sit for 10mins. Enjoy!
