Meatloaf

Meatloaf recipe with the best glaze.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs ground beef, 85% - 90% lean
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 2 large eggs
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 3 tablespoons of ketchup
  • 3/4 cup of Panko breadcrumbs
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons of salt, or to taste
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons of Italian seasoning
  • 1/4 teaspoon of ground black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon of ground paprika
Glaze
  • 3/4 cup of ketchup
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon of white vinegar
  • 2 1/2 tablespoon of brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of onion powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon of ground black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375℉
  2. In a large bowl, add all of the ingredients for the meatloaf. Mix thoroughly.
  3. Add the meat mixture to the loaf pan, gently pressing it down to shape evenly.
  4. Bake for 40mins.
  5. In a small bowl mix together all the ingredientsfor the glaze. Spread the glaze over the top of the meatloaf, continue to cook for a further 15-20mins or until the internal temp is 160℉.
  6. Pull out of oven and let the meatloaf rest for 10mins before serving.
  7. Serve and Enjoy!
